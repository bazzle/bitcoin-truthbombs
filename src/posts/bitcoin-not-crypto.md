# Bitcoin not crypto

It’s as clear as the sky is blue that crypto is a scam-filled cesspit of venture capitalists throwing money at sociopathic founders who see moral obligation towards retail investors or depositors as secondary to becoming filthy stinking rich and powerful. Cory klippstein, in his amazing chat with Preston Pysh, just after the FTX fallout, reveals the absurdity of how venture capitalists gravitate towards crypto vaporware like moths to the flame, and throw money at them even before they have any revenues, market-fit or a working product. The secret sauce for venture capitalists is time to liquidity and ability to “make their own weather”, to hype their product through marketing and controlling the narrative around their product.
