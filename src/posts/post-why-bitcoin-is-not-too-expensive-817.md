# Why bitcoin is not too expensive

Bitcoins “marketing strategy” is its NGU technology.

The idea, from Parker Lewis, that the more expensive bitcoin becomes, the more it gets taken seriously as a money. The more expensive bitcoin becomes, the more value can be transferred per unit.
