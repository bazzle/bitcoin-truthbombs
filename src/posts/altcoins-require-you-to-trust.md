# Altcoins require you to trust

You need to trust them to be moral and not enrich themselves through co-ordinated pumping and dumping. You need to trust them not to change the protocol your money is parked in. Ethereum for example is in a constant state of flux. The founder and his close band of premine beneficiaries disingenuously claim its token can be a store of value, when its inflation rate changes along with the fundamental rules of the protocol.
