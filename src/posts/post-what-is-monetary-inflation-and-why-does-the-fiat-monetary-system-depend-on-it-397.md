# What is monetary inflation and why does the fiat monetary system depend on it

Deficit spending.

How inflation is important for fiat system, because it allows cheaper borrowing. Borrow money at todays prices, pay back tomorrow when the owed amount is worth less.

How deflation is harmful for the fiat system, because it requires more expensive borrowing. Borrow money at todays prices, pay back tomorrow when the owed amount is worth more.
